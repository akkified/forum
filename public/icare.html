Integrity

We show integrity by following the correct rules and protocols, and making sure our users do the same. It inspires both students and tutors alike to show integrity, as we encourage honest and ethical behavior here at Luminous.


Curiousity

Our forum inspires students attain curiousity and be curious. It encourages them to ask and answer questions over various academic topics.


Advocacy

Advocacy is prevalent in our forum in the way students can support themselves and others. Our forum can motivate others to try and find out how to solve a problem instead of simply looking it up. We also display advocacy by promoting people to show the method used when solving instead of just posting an answer.


Resiliency

Our members show resiliency by actively attempting to improve at various subjects through our programs instead of staying at their current skill level.


Empathy

Our members show empathy by going out of their way to help other students. This in turn creates a positive environment not only at school, but also online.
